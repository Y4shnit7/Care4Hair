What is Care4Hair?

Care4Hair offers an online hair loss diagnosis service through a free hair test co-developed with medical professionals and advanced AI technology.Our online platform enables us to identify the root cause of your hair loss and create a personalised treatment plan tailored to your specific hair fall concerns. The online nature of our service provides convenience and accessibility for our users.

How long does is take to see results by using our recommended products?

The time frame for witnessing results can vary but typically falls within 5-6 months for Stages 1 and 2 of the hair loss cycle. Several factors, including the type of hair loss, hair loss stage, age, root causes, and more, influence the duration of the treatment process. Care4Hair's comprehensive approach to addressing hair fall involves gradual improvement in various aspects of your health, paving the way for regrowth.

Are there any side effects with Care4Hair's product?

Care4Hair's products are carefully formulated to be safe, with no reported side effects. However, individual responses may vary, and if you have specific concerns, our team of hair coaches is available to provide guidance and address any issues. Safety and effectiveness are paramount in our product development.
